    Press Play On Tape is a multidisciplinary sound
    studio based in Paris operating at the crossroads
    of sound design and composition. It was founded
    a few years ago by Loïc Bénart,known to some as a
    friend, to others as a dad and to most fellows as
    “the sound guy.”

    The studio works alongside innovative brands
    and institutions to help them tell their story,
    shape ideas and gather communities through audio
    branding,production, or basically anything where
    sound matters.
